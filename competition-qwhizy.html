Quran Whiz
Qwhizy

"Quran Whiz" is a name that combines the words "Quran" and "Whiz." The Quran is the holy book in Islam containing teachings and guidance, while "Whiz" suggests that the application is designed to help users become experts or skilled in memorizing the Quran. This application is a platform for memorizing the Quran based on Android, implementing the takrir method to facilitate the memorization process. Through an educational game approach, the application not only makes the memorization experience more interactive and enjoyable but also enhances the interest of the younger generation in memorizing the Quran.
